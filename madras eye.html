CONJUCTIVITIES(MADRAS EYE):
Conjunctivitis or pink eye is a common condition that spreads rapidly from one person to another.It is often caused by a bacterial or viral infection.
Though not a serious condition,it spreads like an epidemic due to carelessness on the part of the person who suffers from it and the people around.
Allergic conjunctivitis and conjunctivitis due to chemicals or irritants are also types of conjunctivitis, but which does not spread from person to person.
