NECK PAIN:
Neck pain is the sensation of discomfort in the neck area.
Neck pain can result from disorders of any of the structures in the neck, including the cervical vertebrae and
intervertebral discs, nerves, muscles, blood vessels, esophagus, larynx, trachea, lymphatic organs, thyroid gland, or parathyroid glands.
Neck pain arises from numerous different conditions and is sometimes referred to as cervical pain.
Neck pain is medically referred to as cervicalgia.
Neck pain can be associated with
• headaches,
• tingling in the arms,
• muscle pain,
• fever,
• stiff neck,
• throat pain,
• tenderness,
• weakness of the arms.
